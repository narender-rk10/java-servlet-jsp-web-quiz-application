WHY QuizYourIQ???

LEARNING CAN BE FUN

Using QuizYourIQ for review and/or formative assessment is a great way to make learning fun and engaging. Instead of calling on a few students during review, every student is responsible for reviewing, practicing, and learning important information. QuizYourIQ keep student's attention focused on review. QuizYourIQ allow for much easier cumulative review so students don't forget information you want them to know. Create challenges for high score or fastest completion to increase competition between students and award excellence. Great for better, before or after a lesson presentation, and/or at the end of class.
